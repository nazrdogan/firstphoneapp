In order to login to Facebook chat, you will need to use your Facebook(FB) username and not the email address with which you login to your FB account on a browser. You can locate your FB username in a few simple steps as mentioned below:

1) Login to www.facebook.com on your browser
2) Click -> Accounts -> Account Settings
Your username is displayed here.
3) In case you do not have one, click on "change" next to the username field.
4) Follow the instructions to set your username.

Once you set a username, you can use this along with your FB password to login on mundu IM.
